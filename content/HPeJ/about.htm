Human Power eJournal

Human Power eJournal is an open source electronic journal devoted to exploring advancements in human power for transportation and other applications. Human Power eJournal's Creative Commons Licence

The editor and associate editors welcome contributions to Human Power eJournal. Please contact the editorial staff before sending any materials. Articles should be of long term technical interest. Contributions should be understandable by any English-speaker in any part of the world. Units should be in S.I. (with local units optional). Many contributions are sent out for review by specialists. Alas! We cannot pay for contributions. They are, however, extremely valuable for the growth of the human power movement.
