Editorial and article on human-powered chain ferries

Theo Schmidt, December 21st, 2016

Human Power eJournal has received no completed articles suitable for publication over the last two years. With a majority of people worldwide voting with their wallets or their ballots in favour of fossil fuel interests which threaten countless millions of lives, this is no wonder. Also, the members of the Human Power Institute are getting older. As a small sign of life however, here is at least one article: a paper on human-powered chain ferries, as yet only available as PDF.
