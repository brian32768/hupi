Beginnings

Welcome to the first issue of the Human Power eJournal!

Produced by the Human Power Institute, HPeJ is an open source technical journal devoted to exploring the use of the human body as a modern and sophisticated power source for transportation and other uses.

At present we hope to add about one new article per month. You will be given the opportunity to watch and comment as HPeJ grows and matures.

Check back to follow our progress!
