Special Election Editorial

We don't usually do political editorials on HPeJ, but these are desperate times. This is my personal opinion and doesn't reflect the views of other editors or HuPI.  Although the election is an internal US affair, it has a great influence on the whole world, so I feel justified in expressing my view even though I'm not an American.

I think it is extremely important to vote the US-Bush administration out of office. Rather than a long list of reasons, let me just give a couple:

Prerequisite to human power is of course human life. With its illegal war, the Bush administration has destroyed tens of thousands of lives directly. See http://www.iraqbodycount.net . Instead of removing terrorism, the Bush policies have resulted in more terrorism. In order to reverse the trend, not just the symptoms but the reasons for terrorism must be combatted. Only a new US administration can do this.

The war has much to do with oil and the heavy use of oil is also killing millions of people indirectly through air pollution and climate change effects. A new US administration will not be able to change this much but will certainly give better support to renewable energies - including human power.

If you have US voting rights it is very important that you go and vote! Please don't waste your vote! If you don't know how or still need to register, go visit http://www.rockthevote.com .  In many states it may be too late, but in some you have until October 18th to register.

Do it now!

Theo Schmidt, Editor
