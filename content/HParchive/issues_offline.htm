23 Aug 2004 bwilson Once again I have unintentionally ruffled feathers amongst the HPVA board (North American chapter of IHPVA) by linking to copies of PDF's that are already available to the public on the World Wide Web. As far as I know this is only a problem in the United States; check with your local IHPVA chapter for more information, or check Google. I am respecting the request of HPVA president Paul Gracey and have removed the links.
