> My new pedal boat "FAST 3" is now great at wave  hanging on the wake of passing river pleasure boats.
> The following mods. have been undertaken;
> 1.     2 off ball bearing races fitted to prop strutt.
> 2.     New flat bottomed out riggers set to be just clear of the water have greatly improved the stability.
> 3.      Simple GPS fitted, but indicates very slow disappointing speed
> 4.    Prop calibrated, indicates 20% slipbased on pitch helix with no allowance for angle of attack

> 6.    Rudder still to be sorted after many attemps.
> 7.     Folding prop is working well shedding weed, but failed after I hit a rock as it jammed with one blade shut.
