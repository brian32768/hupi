it to target weight:
http://www.rickwill.bigpondhosting.com/swt_8m.jpg
I have dubbed it a faux-tri because it is a monohul with outriggers  
underwater but looks like a standard monohull topside.  It has better  
accommodation and is shorter than the previous attempt.  It uses many  
of the ideas I have got from pedal boats.  This is partly why I am  
making a flat panel pedal boat because the solar boat will use this  
method and I want to know what is involved.   With the Mars motor I

batteries would drain quickly at this pace though.  I have priced  
lithium batteries and intend to play around with a small battery on  
one of the pedal boats.  We have a solar boat challenge in Australia  
and I might convert a pedal boat to run in this as an interim step.

I am yet to see if it can be built within the weight target.
