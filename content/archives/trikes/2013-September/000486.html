I'm sorry, I don't understand the term "recs."  I'm not familiar with  
the OT ELF, either - I'll just assume it is similar to most tadpoles.  
Part of a junked rear derailleur can be hacked to do the basic job,  
but trike chains are subject to side forces and shaking that can  
throw a chain off, so it helps to add guides to the roller cage.   
Standard 8t derailleur jockey pulleys are high-friction.  If there is  
room for larger idlers, that is better, as are ball bearing centers.
