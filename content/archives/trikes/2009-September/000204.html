Hi Jim

One thing that I did not see mentioned yet is the pedal to pedal spacing and
the pedals type.

 The first change I made to help with knee pain on GS-GTO is to use
Speedplay Frog pedals which allow a large rotation. Next I found help from
using pedal extenders -- using 20mm.  I notice that my feet have to angle
with toes out on the trike and my heel would strike the inner part of the
pedal without extenders.  I am still using the original cranks supplied by
Greenspeed -- I think they are 170mm.

With DF bike, I do not have any of these problems and use Shimano M959 pedal
which has a limited rotation.

I fitted my wife's TT3.6 with 155mm cranks, Speedplay Frog pedals and 20mm
pedal extenders -- this fixed knee pain for her.

Bob
