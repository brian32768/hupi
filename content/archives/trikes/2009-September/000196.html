I have Hope Minis on my Trice Micro and once had trouble with the 
brakes sticking on - the pads were not retracting properly - I (several 
times) took the calipers off and cleaned around the pistons before 
forcing (VERY CAREFULLY) them back - each time the brakes seemed to 
work, but as soon as I started riding, they would stick on again - 
eventually I realised it was the was brake levers that were sticking - 
a drop of oil on each lever pivot and everything worked perfectly again.
