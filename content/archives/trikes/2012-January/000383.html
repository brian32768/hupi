Hi Fran,

You are going to be wanting 47-406 Marathon Plus (just a little short of 2in wide). Probably as well to remember to add new tubes too if the existing tyres have rotted.

I have the smaller 35-406 and they are fine but a bit harsh.


HS348 is the tyre/tread name on the other tyres - just an alternative to saying 'Marathon Plus' or whatever on them.
