Hope produces a specific fluid for their brakes.  It is not the  classic 
corrosive brake fluid used in cars.  I think it is mineral-oil  based, but I 
would have the local bike shop fill, bleed and service the  brakes.  I I 
liked the Hopes I had on my first tandem much more than the  Maguras that GS 
went to.  We recently had another trike built up locally,  and the shop used 
the cable actuated Avid 7s. I specified these to avoid  problems  while on 
long trips in the "boonies".. The feel is not quite as  good as the 
hydraulics,but now I don'tworry about leaks or nicked hoses.
