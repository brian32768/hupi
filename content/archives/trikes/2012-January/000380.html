HI Fran,

You'll want to check which of the 20" sizes you actually have.  There's 406mm, 419mm and 451mm, all of which are considered 20".  I'm guessing it's actually 20x1.75, which suggests to me that you're looking for the 406mm size.  You may want to check the actual bead seat diameter, if your rims aren't marked.  Checkout http://sheldonbrown.com/tire-sizing.html.  You could also contact Greenspeed.


"HS348" is the series number for Schwalbe Marathon Plus.
