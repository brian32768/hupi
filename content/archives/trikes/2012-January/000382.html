Hi Fran,

I'm trying to remember what I use on the Hope brakes - I think it is just light mineral oil - the bottle I have to hand is actually Singer sewing machine oil. This job is most easily done by a bike shop tho - I don't like adjusting the hydraulic brakes if I don't have to. A local mountain bike shop should be able to work the brakes for you for maybe $20 - they are just the same as mountain bike brakes.

I'd expect the Rohloff & Schlumpf to just need some exercise... As long as they had oil coverage when they went away then I'd hope for them still to be sealed and ready to go. A routine oil change on both would be a good precaution but I'd give them a try first. You might have more problems with the drive chain though, depending on the conditions around where the bike has been stored - sometimes they rust solid if they weren't well protected before being put away.

Good luck!

Rob
