 I am going to change tires on my GTE as they are dry rotting. Now the
 problem for me is what size. I want to go with Marathon Plus because I
 don't want to change any flats if possible. The tires don't have any
 #'s on them except 20x175. When I look for tires they have other #'s
 for the tire. The tires on the Ute also have a HS348 even though it
 says 20x175 and I don't under stand what all these mean and don't want
 to buy the wrong size tire. Any suggestions?? Thanks Fran
It sounds like what you want is a "406-42" or something close in the
ast two digits.  The 406 corresponds to the 20" diameter of your tire,
n mm.  The 42 corresponds to the 1.75" width of your tire, in mm.
here is actually a decimal point in your 175.
