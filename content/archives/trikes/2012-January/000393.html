I like BB7's on my trike. Only have to adjust them once a season.

The SA 70mm drums work great on the WAW velo. I suspect its because there
are separate Avid Speed Dial levers for each drum.

The same drums were bad on the Go One velo with the puny ganged levers
common on the u-joint tiller steered velos.
