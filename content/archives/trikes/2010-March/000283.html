I have used Scorchers with Kelvar on my Kettwiesel.  No flats in 6000 miles.  On all my recumbents I have always tried to get tires with Kelvar.  When I couldn't I used tire liners.  Over the years I have had very few flats.  I have a 3 speed hub on the rear of my Gold Rush and it makes for real problems if I would have to fix a flat so I am running that with a Slime tube.  One interesting side light is that with the Slime tube I don't have to add air very often.  Kind of nice.
