Thanks for the comment, Bob. 22 lbs? That's a bit too soft for me, 
although I haven't spent a lot of time pumping up the trike tires. I 
generally pump them up to around 75-80 psi; then leave them alone for a 
month or so. Eventually I check and find them @ 40, and then pump them 
up again. Interestingly, the 3 flats followed immediately on the heels 
of my pumping them back up to 80 psi. I guess no surprise there, since 
really hard tires will attack any shard they find along the roadway.
