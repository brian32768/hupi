Last time I wanted to change my bar end levers I bought Shimano down tube levers for GBP30/US45...
They are a little rare but the ones I picked up had the same type of click-ring, fitted straight on to the bar end lever mounts and the levers themselves were all metal and much nicer. Watch out tho as some have curved click rings that wrap around the downtube.
