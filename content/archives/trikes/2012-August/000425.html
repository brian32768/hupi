The other day, after a fairly short 10 mile ride on my GTO, I noticed a 
chafing on my bottom, in the center, just above my sit bones. And the 
next morning, after scratching the area (because it had become itchy), I 
discovered that my underpants were extremely bloody. I have previously 
felt chafing in the same spot, but it never was this bad, and it never 
before bled.

I may have an explanation for this unpleasant condition: a year ago, the 
elastic that held the mesh seat in place broke, and I repaired it with 
some bungee cord I purchased from a sports shop. However, I don't think 
I did a great job: the mesh eventually folded over itself in a couple of 
places, INCLUDING EXACTLY WHERE THE CHAFING OCCURRED. A photo of the 
seat (showing the wrinkling) is attached.

Has anyone else had the same problem? For the time being, my "cure" is 
to cover the seat with some yoga mat rubber material . . . and it seems 
to have worked. And my next venture is to just purchase a new mesh seat. 
But I'm curious to see if anyone else has had the same problem.
