I'm cycling in Ride Ataxia on March 23, 2012. Ride Ataxia offers route lengths for all cycling abilities with a 6 mile, a 25 mile and a 50 mile course. Join me for a great ride with beautiful North Texas views and for thebest post-ride food around, catered by Outback Steakhouse on the grounds on UNT's Apogee Stadium. And it is all for a great cause- the Friedreich's Ataxia Research Alliance (FARA). 

FARA is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing research to bring treatments forward for Friedreich's ataxia (FA). FA is a progressive, life shortening neuromuscular disease, and FARA is the largest funder of FA research in the US. To learn more about FARA and the children and adults living with FA visit: www.cureFA.org

Hope to see you on the road!
