Thanks Giles,

I cut the fabric, and the grommets are mostly not touching steel now.  A couple are close.  I could make them further but I didn't want to cut the fabric past the hem until seeing if others are cut past the hem.

At $100+ for new fabric, I don't want to cut it too far.

Notice this persons picture shows a gap in the seat fabric where the horizontal supports go?

http://grothserver.princeton.edu/~groth/random_pics/bikerides/trike_20050620/TRIKE_20050620_17.htmlx

There were not gabs in mine and the grommets really ground into the frame.  The other GTR's I have seen all had gaps or cuts to go around the seat frame.  I have been very happy with my dealer and the service, but a few items on assembly were not quite done with the care of others I have seen.

You have a good point though, my fabric has an 8.5 inch gab on the back side......others look to be closer to 5-6 inches.  Maybe the fabric is a bit too small.

http://grothserver.princeton.edu/~groth/random_pics/bikerides/trike_20050620/TRIKE_20050620_18.htmlx

Those pictures are not of mine, just the first pics I found on google of greenspeed seats from the underside.
