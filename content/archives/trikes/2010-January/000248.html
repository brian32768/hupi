Hi Chris,
               The eyelets should NOT touch the seat frame. How this
has occured I don't know. Maybe you could send me some photos
so I could see why this has occured and we can prevent it from
happening again.

The latter GTOs which have the Ergo seats have the fabric slit so that
 the fabric follows the contours of the seat rails, whereas on the earlier
GTOs with the flat seat backs, it was not slit so that you got some
lumber support from where the seat brace was not gathering the material.

You could possibly slit the material, and when you re-lace the seat you
could cut the shock cord into a number of pieces to adjust the tension
in the cords to suit the shape of your back.
