Hi Jules,

I found good quality cables with continuous PTFE liners fixed this problem - this keeps out the moisture which can then freeze.
Gore Ride-On are maybe the leading brand but at the highest price. I have successfully used Nokon and SRAM Flak Jacket. 
Look in the mountain bike section of your preferred bike shop/web site.

And, at least here in the UK where temps only drop a few degrees below freezing, hydraulic disc brakes do not seem to suffer from this problem - I'm assured they're good to at least -10C.

The no-cost solution is to ensure the trike is kept in a damp and condensation free environment - but this isn't always practical if it is your daily commuter!!!
