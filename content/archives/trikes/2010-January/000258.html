I find seat fabric wears pretty well too, I use the stuff that outdoor 
furniture is made from, and brass grommets. The seat is usually a simple 
rectangle. Cutting slots out for tubes, etc. in my experience leads to 
weakness.

One tip I've found - brass grommets have a smooth side and a rough side 
when they're installed. Resist the temptation to put the smooth side out 
where it can be seen - then the rough side rubs up against the 
overlapping fabric on the seat, and eventually cuts through it.
