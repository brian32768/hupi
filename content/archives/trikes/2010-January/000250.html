I used this 'PVC Mesh Tarp' from Northern Tool to replace my  Greenspeed 
seat. I see they now have Nylon mesh as well. I did not use brass  eyes, just 
sewed hems on both sides and cut slits to form "loops" to run the  bungy 
cord to pull the material taunt. It has held up for several years, but not  as 
well as the original seat material. I interlaced heavy shoe string over the  
bungy cord in some areas to keep the seat more firm.
