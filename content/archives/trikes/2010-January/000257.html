I second Mike Ross' comments about dealing with Greenspeed. They are 
simply superb. A few years ago, the SRAM Dual Drive hub on my GTO  
failed, after less than 4,000 miles. I called SRAM and they blamed it on 
the trike, which made no sense at all. When I told this to Paul Sims, he 
immediately sent me a replacement hub and even though I offered to pay 
for it -- after all, it wasn't Greenspeed's fault -- he would not accept 
payment for it. AND I HAD PURCHASED THE GTO USED!

So not only do I love my trike, I love the company that made it too.
