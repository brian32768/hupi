Interesting that they are using an external controller for the Cyclone motor.

The normal Cyclone controller within the motor limits the amps to 30 amps.

The company who supplied us with the motors, and used the motors on their
own trikes, said that with heavy use, the motor magnets de-magnatized.
I have not had any problems with de-magnatization, but I've only
been using the internal controllers and 24 volts (max 26.5 volts) which
restricts to input to about 800 watts.  

If you want power assistance on your GTC, I would suggest the German
Hienxmann hub motor which can be bought in a number of different 
gear box and and power configerations.

We are now working on a dedicated assited trike, using the Cyclone motor
with automatic transmission.
