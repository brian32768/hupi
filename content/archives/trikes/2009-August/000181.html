B D wrote:
> I believe that a slightly rising linkage combined with a disc brake for extra locking can be very interesting and add advantage over other designs. The system you describe that is rising near the center and then falls is very interesting. Are there any descriptions on how to build this or other rising linkages?

The one I've seen has the usual parallelogram linking the two rear 
wheels. Instead of the frame just pivoting at the centre of one of the 
horizontal members, it has a cam that rolls along it. The shape of this 
cam determines how the weight rises. I can't find a decent drawing of 
the setup, unfortunately.

Some really good info at http://www.jetrike.com/prior-art.html on 
different designs that have been tried. I like the one that can be 
ridden down stairs and still stay upright :-)
