Very clever, indeed! It is so great that this design seems to work well.

The old european cars with rear beam axle I referred to eariler did not have
watts link from factory, it could be bought from hot tuning shops. The
original solution on these cars was "panhard" or transversal bar, a simpler
solution than the watts link with only two bearings but it did cause the
rear axle to move slightly sideways during spring movement.
