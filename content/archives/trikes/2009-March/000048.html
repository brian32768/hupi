I have deliberately left out all gussets (except a pair at the top of the
'T' of the front suspension arm). I want to see if anything on this
prototype breaks. 

You can also argue that the rear spaceframe needs more bracing, and that the
frame Watts Link support should be braced to the upper spring mounts. All
make sense; all would add weight.

Re flipping it over - I have been giving this one some 'dan testing' and if
you yank on full lock at speed, it can still occur.
