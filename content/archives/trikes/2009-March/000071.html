Before I built our greenspeed GTEs we tried every seat angle we could 
find, and settled on 43 Deg.
 This may not be the most aero of positions, but its comfortable and 
gives a good view.
 I found the more laid back positions gave me a sore neck as my chin was 
as low as I could get it (if I relaxed I was looking at sky)
 This is the opposite of wedgie touring (relax and your staring at the 
road).
 
Cheers
