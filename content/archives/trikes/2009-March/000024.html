It's a joke, right?

It looks just like any other amateur first attempt at a recumbent
trike, albeit one with some design flair and made of carbon fibre.
That actually seems to be the target market - people who want it to
look nice, rather than those who want to go fast or carry anything.

appear to have any way to carry more than a wallet and cellphone.

That said, I'd love to test one... mostly because if some 102kg guy
has had a real go at it I'd like to know if I can meet the challenge.
Looking at it I suspect that heavy stop-start riding would see the
end of it within a Mm or two. But it would be fun to find out.
