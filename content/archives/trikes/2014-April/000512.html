I've never used chain tubes, but their power draw should be easy to  
calculate from the slope that they will slide on, their weight, and  
the chain speed.  I think that windage is negligible, since the feet  
are moving faster with any practical chainring size.
One possible problem with higher chain speeds is that the lowered  
tension does allow more sag from gravity, giving another source of  
"frame flex" pedal softness.  More or better spaced idlers may be  
indicated.

Bob
