Yeah - it’s the length between the bolt and top jockey wheel that you need to look at….
That top length needs to be tuned to the largest sprocket - the B-screw only fine tunes that distance.
“Road” mechs are short in this dimension (aimed at, what 11-23, 11-26 cassettes), MTB mechs tend to be longer (aimed at max 32, 34, 36t sprockets).
Then you select the lower cage length for the amount of take-up you need...
