Well, in theory that might be the case, but with the revival of some of the 
tilting delta designs, that is pretty much no longer the case.  I made a 
narrow track FWD tilting delta and it's teh fastest trike I've ever ridden 
or raced, by far.  The tilting deltas corner like a 2 wheeler with none of 
the loss associated with hard cornering on a tadpole.  They also have none 
of the stability concerns of standard deltas, and finally, with a 25º seat 
angle and the rear wheels tucked in behind the seat for maximum aero 
benefit, they have a better aero profile than any conventional delta or 
tadpole out there.
