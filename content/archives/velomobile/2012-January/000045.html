    Congratulations on the new group.
After hosting four years of the Niagara Velomobile  Happening I have a few
 observations to  share.
    We have had a wide range of velomobiles show up and endless technical
discussion
going into the late night hours.
   We have had enjoyable rides through town and displayed our velomobiles.
What I observe from this wide range of designs  is that they all work and
provide the
riders with satisfactory transportation.
   Every possible need or solution has been addressed by someone. There have
been clever solutions to perceived problems that may not exist.
   I take enjoyment from building velomobiles and using them but actually
selling them is a chore for which I am not qualified.
    Production since 1990, twenty one years ,has been one to two units per year.


     Perhaps a marketing movement will emerge in this group to move us foreward.
