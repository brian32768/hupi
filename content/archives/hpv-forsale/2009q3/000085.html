This FastBack Carbon hydration pack is the earlier version of the  
current FastBack C100 hydration pack. It straps to hardshell seats  
such as M5, Optima, Volae and Bacchetta. The pack fits in the middle  
between the two ribs on the back of the seat and takes a 2-litre  
hydration bladder such as Camelbak, Nalgene or Platypus (bladder not  
included). The C100 is $56 plus shipping and I'm selling this Carbon  
hydration pack for less than half price at $27 plus postage. It is in  
excellent condition and the only reason I'm selling it is because the  
tube from the bladder exits on the right side. All my other bikes  
have C100 or 70LS hydration packs where the tube exits on the left  
side and to avoid confusion I have standardised all my bikes on left  
side hydration tubes.
