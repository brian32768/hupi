I have one pair of Greenspeed Scorcher folding 40-406 (20x1.5")  
tyres. These were designed to have the lowest rolling resistance of  
any 406 mm tyre and this is the folding version which is extremely  
light for such a wide tyre. I weighed one of them at 278 g and the  
other at 281 g. Black with a checker pattern reflective sidewall  
stripe on each side. I used these for a week of commuting and decided  
I prefer the better wet weather traction of Schwalbe Kojaks. These  
would be excellent tyres for someone who wants to go fast with  
minimal energy on rough roads and only rides in dry conditions. They  
have 282.4 km (175.088 miles) on them. The front tyre (281 g) doesn't  
show any signs of wear. The rear tyre (278 g) has several tiny  
superficial nicks in the tread. $20.50 each plus postage.
