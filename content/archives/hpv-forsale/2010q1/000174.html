Used Shimano 600 headset:
This Shimano 600 HP-6500 1 inch threaded headset is Ultegra level and  
has cartridge bearings. It came on a bike I bought new in June 1999  
and I recently upgraded the fork on this bike and the new fork  
required a threadless headset so I'm selling this Shimano 600  
headset. It has been ridden about 6000 km mostly in dry weather and  
was still turning just as smoothly as when it was new when I took  
took it out of service. The cartridge bearings are readily available  
from Shimano and I believe this model headset is still in production.  
$35.

Nightsun light parts:
I was cleaning out my lighting collection and found the following  
surplus Nightsun parts:

2 new SunSport 6V 6W sealed bulb-reflector units, retails for $19.95,  
will sell these for $10 each.

1 new TriLight 12V 7.5W MR11 sealed bulb-reflector, retails for

2 Prostick 9-LED taillights. These were the first high intensity

plastic tube contains 9 LEDs which are steady (no flashing mode).
