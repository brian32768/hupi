I received some Panaracer Minits Lite folding 32-406 (AKA 20x1.25")  
tyres. These are made in Japan and appear to be very high quality.  
They are all black with completely smooth treads and thin, supple  
casings so they look like they will have low rolling resistance,  
perhaps at the expense of durability. The claimed weight is 180 g and  
I weighed one at 181 g and another at 182 g. For comparison I've  
weighed Schwalbe Durano folding 28-406 tyre at 170 to 181 g. This  
makes the Panaracer Minits Lite the second lightest 406 mm tyre  
available and the lightest in the 32 mm width. $40 plus postage. To  
arrange purchase, e-mail me at the address in my signature below.
