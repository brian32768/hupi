In 2003 I bought two new sets of Magura Marta disc brakes. These were  
among the lightest weight disc brakes available at the time and are  
still lighter than most disc brakes currently on the market. I bought  
them for a project bike I never ended up going through with. I don't  
currently have a use for them and am trying to consolidate my brake    
collection to one shelf so am offering these for sale.

One brake has a right lever and the other has a left lever. Both have  
black levers and calipers with 160 mm International Standard 6-bolt  
rotors. Both calipers are of the International Standard 51 mm hole  
spacing direct mount type. Both are new in boxes.

I am selling them for $150 each plus shipping if they need to be  
shipped.
