2008 Sun Tomahawk, $595 plus shipping

For photos, please see:
http://www.easystreetrecumbents.com/used__bikes/TOMAHAWK.html

Standard parts including SRAM SX4 shifters and RD, Shimano FD, Avid 
Single Digit 3 rear brake, Avid FR-5 levers, and a Tektro U-brake that 
you can replace for $15 extra (it isn't very strong). Maximum 
recommended rider weight is 225#.

Seat and grips are in good conditions. Sun's seat isn't 
super-lightweight, but it is quite comfortable with its adjustable 
lumbar support and thick base cushion. If you like the seat on your EZ-1 
or EZ-Sport and want something lighter, this is a good choice.

3x8 gearing with 700C drive wheel for high speeds. 406 front wheel 
offers a wide variety of tire choices. Handlebar riser has adjustable 
tilt with a locking pivot and a wide range of height adjustment, 
allowing for a wide range of rider size and seat recline.

Accessories include Greenfield kickstand, 2x Mirrycle mirrors, HB water 
bottle cage, and springer rear rack.
