I  have one new, never installed RANS Hoagie seat for sale for $350.  
This seat is similar in configuration to a Bacchetta Euromesh seat  
but the frame is CrMo steel rather than aluminium, it is slightly  
wider and the base has a different shape which several riders have  
told me they find more comfortable than the Euromesh. The brackets on  
the base of the seat have the same spacing as a Bacchetta seat mount  
so this seat will fit right on to any Bacchetta bike using the  
standard Bacchetta Delrin slider. The Hoagie seat will also fit any  
newer RANS bike using the RANS V-Clamp mount. The geometry of the  
seat is designed for a high bottom bracket, laid back position so it  
works best on the SWB bikes from RANS and Bacchetta. I weighed the  
Hoagie seat at 1484 grams with pad and 1240 grams without pad. The  
pad isn't necessary to ride it.

The prices are as follows:
New RANS Hoagie seat with pad $350 plus postage
Slightly used RANS V-Clamp for 2" top tube $35 plus postage
New RANS telescoping seat strut set $30.00 plus postage

I will sell any of these parts separately.
