This used Tracer 155 mm crankset came off an HP Velotechnik Scorpion.  
It is black and has a single black 52 tooth 130 mm BCD chainring on  
it with a black 130 mm BCD guard ring mounted to each side of the  
chainring. The spider on this crankset is 130 mm BCD only, it does  
not have a 74 mm BCD set of holes for mounting a smaller inner  
chainring. The smallest chainring this crankset will take is 38  
teeth. It is in good condition other than a minor scrape on the edge  
of the outer guard ring. As configured this crankset would be great  
for single chainring applications such as on a bike equipped with an  
internally geared hub or a single speed but it could also be set up  
with two chainrings or with extra long chainring bolts and spacers  
with three chainrings as long as the smallest ring isn't any smaller  
than 38 teeth. $50 plus postage.
