Bonjour à tous,
Voici des nouvelles du comité des records de la WHPVA où deux nouveaux
records d'endurance viennent d'être reconnus.

Félicitations à Guy Martin et Jason Miles !

Jean-Charles Gosselin

"On behalf of the WHPVA and much to my pleasure I can hereby confirm
that the WHPVA record committee has accepted the record attempt
carried out by Guy Martin and Jason Miles at Goodwood Circuit,
Chichester, West Sussex, UK. They have covered 509.84km in 12 hours
and 909.79km in 24 hours.
Congratulations!

The new world record will be published on the WHPVA website, all
material sent in will be made available to the public and a
certificate is going to be sent to both riders.

Kind regards,

Edgar Teufel"
