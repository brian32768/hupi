Je viens de trouver chez mon libraire "L'index" (Coiffard) à Nantes un  
savoureux livre sur la culture vélo.

"VELO bicycle culture and design" (collectif) publié en anglais par  
GESTALTEN en allemagne  ISBN 978 3 89955 284 3

C'est plein de photos de vélos inspirés et inspirateurs !

www.gestalten.com

http://www.gestalten.com/books/detail? 
id=ceafb21a2721f16b012723cde6990004

Velo introduces a wild bunch of passionate cyclists – frame builders,  
urban planners, artists, photographers, and those who ride  
professionally – who are making an impact. They are not only shaping  
styles, but promoting cycling as a primary form of transport. The book  
also explores the aesthetic of today’s cycling culture and presents  
custom-made frames and art bikes as well as a selection of contemporary  
illustration and design influenced by the cycling movement. Geared  
toward anyone who has a personal or professional interest in cycling,  
Velo is the fast lane into a current topic that is both entertaining  
and socially relevant.
