I guess it is time for an update. See 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Human_Powered_Vehicle_Association
for a current description and link list, although this can change any time. 
Basically the old sites are at whpva.org and hpva.us, respectively. HPVA has 
renamed and grabbed ihpva.org. Warren Beauchamp has assembled some old and new 
content there. Warren's recumbents.com site has all the gory details in the 
forum. The Human Power Archive conflict seems resolved and safe either way. 
ICANN is still trying to decide whether the domain name hijack was proper or 
not.  Neil Carter (BHPC) will take over from Brian Wilson whether for ihpva.org 
or whpva.org. We don't yet know what to do with the Wikis, as they use LAMP 
(Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP) and BHPC uses Windows thingies. For the English Wikis 
this hardly matters, but the French one has a bit of useful stuff.

Cheers, Theo
