I was delighted to see this show give high profile attention to recumbents, the Delft team and Battle Mountain, and the White Zombie electric car.  All very cool technologies that impress even the hard core conventionals that dominate our society (when has it been different?).  Heck, 83 mph even impresses roadies.

Highly recommended.

And the sailing shots were spectacular!  It's interesting to me how the America's cup rules have evolved over the years to accommodate the most radical of sailing designs, while the UCI has stagnated for many decades.  Having the winner decide the rules seems to have made a radical difference in approach.
