Hi Eric.  I have had lots of bikes and trikes over the years.  I started with a Rans V-Rex Back in 1995.  I slowly aged and in 2011 I went total trike.  The Cat trikes and other tadpoles are hard to get out of.  My suggestion is a Delta.  I have a Hase and love it.  (I also still ride a TerraTrike).  At one point I put a Electric Wheel on my Hase.  It was great.   On the other hand an EZ-3 would be less expensive and would be a good commute bike as you are only going 7 miles each way.  It is also a little higher.  

I sent you an email with a couple of pictures and then remembered most people don’t open email with attachments from people they don’t know.

I would be glad to answer any questions and or send pictures.
