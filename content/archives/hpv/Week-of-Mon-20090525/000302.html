Tom Sherman wrote:
> Any suggestions for alternative clothes for cycling that are more normal 
> appearing and functional off the bike/trike?

Ex Officio makes a nice line of outdoor activity clothes that don't make 
you look like a superhero.  These have all the technical aspects of the 
sports clothes like wicking, UV protection, and lots of sealable pockets.

At the shop, we carry these models:

Men's pants: Amphi shorts
Women's pants: Take Over shorts
Men's shirts: Reef Runner
Women's shirts:  DryFly

More models can likely be found on Ex Officio's website.
