weeks ago.  I put on a 52 tooth Sugino Cycloid, elliptical ring, I once had
on a fixie.  Set it up as close as I could to the Q ring instructions,
given that I only had five options.  Felt smoother, easier on my knees, so
it stays.

vehicle....period.  Now if only the cars would all get melted down for
cookware!
