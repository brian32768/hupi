I think you mean John Schlitter, not Randy. John is by far the stronger of the two brothers and is the one that has set all sorts of ultra records and done RAAM.

I've also tried oval rings, Rotor Cranks, Rotor Q-Rings (still use them), and 22 tooth inner rings (still use them). I found out long that I'm faster on long, hilly rides on a recumbent than an upright because on an upright my speed really drops once I'm having to go into pain management mode. On a short climb I'm still faster on an upright because I can stand.

After selling three of them this year, I recently got an HP Velotechnik Scorpion fs S-Pedelec e-assist trike of my own. I have some experience riding a friends e-assist Easy Racers Gold Rush in the past and that is the only bike I've ever been able to do a sub-4 hour century ride on. People who look at it from an athletic perspective may view it is cheating but if one is riding for transportation or fun there is no such thing as cheating.
