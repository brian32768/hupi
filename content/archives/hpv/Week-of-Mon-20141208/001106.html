Cornel,

After modifying the drive with my system, I had no difficulty climbing long
steep grades with an 11-34T, 9 speed cassette.  The steepest I actually
measured, and rode repeatedly, was a 17% grade.  There are two
limitations/annoyances with rowing up really steep grades.  At 4 mph, the
bike almost stops between strokes, and any hesitation in engagement of the
silent sprag or roller freewheel is amplified the lower your gearing.
