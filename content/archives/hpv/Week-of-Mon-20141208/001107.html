A huge help, which allowed me to climb with my scrawny arms, was the
adjustable arm cable attachment.  By moving the cable down the riser, with
the quick release clamp, I can reduce the ratio of cable pulled by the arms
from 1:1 to 2:1.  This not only makes the "gear ratio" for arm strength to
leg strength more nearly equal, but makes steering at low speed much easier.
