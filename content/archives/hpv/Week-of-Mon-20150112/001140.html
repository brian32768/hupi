I have been a long time reader of this and the some of the related lists 
and value what is said here as each member has an established reputation 
(like visiting long-time friends).

I understand the desire to move this to a new format (although I don't 
dislike this one), but moving it to something associated with BROL if 
possible (as he has a huge, dedicated userbase of mostly like-minded 
people) is FAR preferable than something associated with Facebook, 
Twitter, or similar (based on personal reasons).

A forum format can allow a huge volume of ideas to be present (like 
BROL), but if you don't keep up with the forum they can disappear 
quickly from "easy" discovery. The mailing list format makes sure we can 
see everything, but is really only manageable for limited user input 
because high volume can end up leading to immediate trash-canning of 
email due to lack of time and too many emails (even with a digest ability).

If there was some way to send forum posts to an automatically sorting 
wiki so that long-term retrieval was easy and viable, that would be even 
better - and perhaps lead to fewer reposts by people who didn't know 
that the topic had just been discussed last month (not a problem here). 
There has been much intelligent information shared here over the years 
that, while perhaps saved, is somewhat "lost" due to the difficulty in 
retrieval of the information.
