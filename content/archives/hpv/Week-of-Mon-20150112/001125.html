Goodbye list. :-)

JW and I already talked about it, it was my suggestion.
I have just been dragging my feet for a year or so.

I have to pay a monthly fee to keep a virtual server running to handle
these lists, it takes extra time to run it, and I want to shut it down to
save time and money. The hupi.org web site will migrate to another less
expensive service that we (JW and I) already have online.

Let's see...

Started at Sonoma State around 1990 on some kind of Sun station in Computer
Science department = hpv at sonoma.edu

NeXT cube (pulled from storage) gopher server and email

Sun 68040-based workstation (donated) also first Web server for
www.ihpva.org

1996 - Quit Sonoma State job so moved IHPVA.org to 486 Linux box at
CDS1.net Mailing lists move to bikelist.org

2008 or so?? Bikelist.org shuts down so the lists move to hupi.org at
hostmonster.com to avoid the ongoing ihpva.org/whpva.org political battle.

Hostmonster immediately overloads (too much traffic on lists) so move to
virtual host at Tektonic.net ($30/month)

2015 - without mailing lists to support I can move hupi.org back to
hostmonster ($4/month).
