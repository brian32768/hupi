Is there an email interface to BentRider forums? E.g., can one get emailed updates for a given subforum? Why I ask:

IMHO forums (i.e., websites run with software like vBulletin and phpBB) and maillists (like this) serve different usecases. Relative to me, forums (of which I use several, mostly related to software--e.g., Linux distros) seem "pull": I go there when I have a specific interest or problem. I can subscribe to individual threads, and get updates about them, but can't subscribe (am I missing something?) to an entire forum (e.g., BentRider>Main Category) or subforum/category (e.g., http://www.bentrideronline.com/messageboard/forumdisplay.php?f=21 ).

OTOH, relative to me, maillists are "push": stuff appears in my inbox, I take a look and either read or delete. Perhaps it's just me, but I find that a useful usecase for some domains, e.g., recumbency.
