Oddly enough, we've been having a similar discussion regarding the utility of the forum on the OHPV site: http://www.ohpv.org/forums/ with regard to "other" forums and Facebook/Pinterest/Twitter.  

My 2 cents: Facebook/Pinterest/Twitter are the flavor of the moment, but are owned by entities who probably don't care if we exist or not. If they disappear, (anyone remember MySpace?) our community for another place to gather. There are plenty of forums out there with similar constituencies (Bentrider, Recumbents.com, BikeForums), so perhaps a mass migration is in order. 

I like several features of the RikeForums forum software, but then Recumbents.com is run by one of "us". On the gripping hand, Bentrider seems to have the most active community. 

My suggestion is that we ask Bryan Ball to set up a couple subforums for the IHPVA: one for general business and a second "private" forum for the IHPVA board to mutter amongst themselves. What say ye?
