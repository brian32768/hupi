> People have reported up to 40% better range, but the main benefit would be due
> to less wear on the mechanical brakes (and rims if applicable).

40% is highly optimistic for an ebike.  For a typical trip expect to
recover more like 5-15%, depending on hilliness, number of stops and
starts and controller sophistication or user skill in modulating regen
current.
