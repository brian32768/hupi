Your observations and the interesting link suggest a slight amendment.

"All transportation is intertwined with" how we share public space.

This suggested change comes from experiencing, while aboard a  kayak, 
how a single motor boat operator who ignores or is unaware of no-wake 
zones can take over a public space (e.g.,a water way). This matter of 
maintaining civility between humans who use motors and those humans who 
use muscles for propulsion magnifies when on the water.

Sometimes I think we humans, and the birds too, are just plain lucky 
that swarms of flying cars (motorized or human powered) don't exist, yet.

John Snyder
