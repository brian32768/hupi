You probably know this, but...

That's where capacitors differ from batteries.  Capacitors can be
charged and discharged at much higher rates but have lower
capacities (per weight and per volume).

Ultracapacitors are commonly referenced in electric car design
papers- they can have larger charge/discharge rates than batteries
and better store more power per weight and volume than capacitors.

> That way you could regen into stop signs then power
