The above links to a graph I made in Oct 2006 of an upright bicycle, 
using an iBike data logger and spreadsheet program for the post-ride 
analysis.

The graph's primary purpose is to depict how the Force due to 
acceleration (F accel) varies in response to changes in road slope and 
natural wind speed.  It's useful to regard Fa as a vector with direction 
relative to forward motion indicated by  a positive or negative sign. 
Thus the force due to acceleration serves as either a propulsive force 
or as an additional source of drag.  In this particular example, IIRC 
there had been no active use of the brakes.
