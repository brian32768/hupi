Tom:

As far as I know Steve is no longer providing support for former Rotator customers.

You could probably get all of this made for you by Easy Racers (ER).  Their seat stays use 3/8" solid aluminum rod, and they have clamps to match.

I have a pair of Pursuit seat stays that I cannot now use as they are too short for me.  (A frame modification required that I use longer seat stays.)  The top of the stay is designed to wrap around a 3/4" seat frame tube.

http://bit.ly/fSStcT

They're used and scratched in a few places, but they are not cracked or bent.  I can sell these to you for $50/pair, which is about half (IRRC) what ER charged me to weld up a new pair.

I don't have any machined clamps to spare, but I'm pretty sure ER has a stock of these and that they would sell you a pair.  ER typically use these with a 1/4-20 bolt, although that would fit an M5 bolt loosely.  They may have un-drilled clamps, too.  Just confirm the dimensions of the rod and bolt when you order.
