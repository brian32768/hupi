Hi Zach,
A quick search online finds many available 451 tires, but you're right, not
as many as 406.  There's been a lot of debate over on the BROL forum about
which is better. Some people have found the 451 rolls a little faster and
smoother on bumpy roads than the 406, apparently.
http://www.bentrideronline.com/messageboard/showthread.php?t=44041&page=2
Interestingly, QBP (one of our suppliers) lists the 406 as for "BMX" and the
451 as for "recumbents". The final specs of our bikes are determined by
Cruzbike designer John Tolhurst, working with our manufacturing partners in
Taiwan. I'll pass your suggestions on to John about using 406 rims. For the
current production Quest, the wheels are packed with the bike, not sold
separately. For someone intent on using 406 rims, they could move the SRAM
Dual Drive hub (and disk brakes) over to a 406 rim as long as the rim had
the proper number of spoke holes. Thanks for your comments.
