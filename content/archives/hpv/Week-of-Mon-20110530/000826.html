Hi Derryl,
I'd be remiss in not letting you know that we recently are "in-stock" with
the Cruzbike Quest 2.0. This is a foldable recumbent that comes either with
dual 20" or 26" wheels.

The 20" version can be packed in a Samsonite F'Lite 31" suitcase.  I'm not
aware if the other models you mention can be, or what their list price is.
The Quest is $1995.

The 2.0 comes with rear rack, elliptical chainring, and a SRAM Dual Drive II
system (internally geared hub) to provide 27 gears.

The 2.0 also has additional QR levers and thumb knobs to make take down of
the bike faster than than the 1.0 version.

It's a rugged bike with dual suspension. We are located in Lumberton, NC
right off I-95. This would be "by appointment only". No dealers in the area
have this bike in stock.

Our website is undergoing a major update, so not all of our info about this
great little bike is up-to-date.
