In lay person's terms, a bike with a big front wheel feels "more  
stable" than a bike with a small front wheel.  They attribute this to  
gyroscopic effect. Then people say that doesn't enter in and say, the  
geometry is wrong on the small wheel bike, then this study says it  
isn't geometry either...  ;-)

Gyroscopic effect may not be the determinant of stability for a  
riderless bike, but it may affect the feel of a bike with a rider.   
Seems like rotating mass is going to have _some_ effect somewhere.
