Fellow recumbent riders,

Please, keep 3-5 July 2009 free in your calender to attend the World
Championship in Tilburg, The Netherlands. General information about
the event can be found on http://wk2009.ligfiets.net/ The races are
described in http://www.ligfiets.net/nieuws/bericht.php3?id=2739&volledig=1

The event is organised by the Netherlands Association for Human
Powered Vehicles (NVHPV) under the auspices of the IHPVA
(http://www.whpva.org/).
