Mike,
The point is we haven't tried enough things under the pressure of race 
conditions to settle on a design, and the bike that performs best in race 
conditions may not do the best in the rat race.

Being a transportation cyclist I'd be more in favor of having road rallies 
and utility cycling events with lots of locations, lots of participants and 
lots of prizes (that fit the accomplishment - like a race for the groceries 
where you get groceries for a prize).

I'm not qualified to say what would happen if we tried to break in to UCI 
where we're not wanted (again).  And I'm not interested after having been in 
a couple of their silly time trials.
Nick
