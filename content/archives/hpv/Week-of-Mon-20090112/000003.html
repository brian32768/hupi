Stuart, et al,
Here's a way everyone can support this initiative on an ongoing basis. 
There's a local swap meet near Pittsburgh, PA coming up and I'm going to 
talk about velomobiles and streamliners.  The organizer was really excited 
about the topic - even though he's an avid transportation and racing cyclist 
nobody's told him what's going on in unrestricted cycling development.  The 
theme for my talk is going to be that racing dogma has suppressed or 
marginalized diversity and practicality of design, but now that's coming to 
an end because of the need for everyone to access sustainable 
transportation.
I would suggest to anyone who is going to be attending a bike show or 
similar event, to get on the schedule to talk about velomobiles and 
streamliners.  If you can't find 20minutes worth of fascinating stuff give 
me a call and I'll be happy to help.

Nick Hein
Morgantown, WV
