Theo said:
> The points are not only 56k Modems but general resource efficiency,

Specifically, the attention of users is limited and valuable. Your ten
seconds spent ensuring an appropriate subject line, proof reading,
tidying quotes and making sure your message is easy to read means that
every single list member saves a few seconds when considering your
message. Multiply that by 500-odd list members and you're saving the
rest of us a combined 30 minutes for your ten seconds work. Not to
mention that you're much more likely to get an answer to any
questions you might ask since people will grow to associate your
address with readable messages.

Or to put it another way, every rude or lazy so-and-so who can't be
bothered doing so costs the group a lot more time than they save
themselves.

Also consider those of us not on the digest who have filters to
automatically delete any message with "Digest" in the subject... if
you can't be bothered with your own message, why should I?
