Slap on a couple of beefy BMX brakes on the trailer, set up the cable so
that it can be removed (tandem cables may be long enough), find a brake
handle where the clamp opens up to make it easy on/off.

The worry is cable stretch, to be practical for really heavy loads it may
take using hydraulics to get the pull back to where it's split by cables to
the two sides. Still worth the trouble but the brake handle with an opening
clamp may be harder to find.
