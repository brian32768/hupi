participants from around Europe. I put quotes around "race" because the

energy, human muscle, and use electric drive trains and other innovative 
approaches which list members may find fascinating.

The following web site is fairly extensive. Navigation links appear on 
the left hand sidebar. A good page to start from is 
http://www.windenergyevents.com/FachhochschuleFlensburg.html

The level of innovation exhibited is awesome.
