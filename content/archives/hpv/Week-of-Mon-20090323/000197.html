Found it! :-)
It was Andreas Könekamp from TU Darmstadt. There was a presentation of the
ideas and principles in "Human Power" no 47
(http://www.whpva.org/HParchive/issues.htm), and the same presentation is
available from his homepage at
http://koenekamp.bluehash.de/bicycle/PhantomTrailer.html (there's also a
German version).

It's very similar to the Berkeley trailer, but has a different hitch and
uses a brake instead of braking with the motor. A prototype(?) was made and
presented at a bicycle fair. The press release, with photos of the trailer,
is here: http://koenekamp.bluehash.de/bicycle/PressReport.html
