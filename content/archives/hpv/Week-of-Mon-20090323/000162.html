That result is wrong.

My initial error was incorrectly asserting, s' = s". If the value of 
time (t) is changed, which alters the value of acceleration, then it 
also alters the distance (s) involved. As, s = t * (Vo + Vf)/2  The 
correct result  is E' = E". 

A longer time for an acceleration does not change the ENERGY input. It 
reduces the POWER requirement.
