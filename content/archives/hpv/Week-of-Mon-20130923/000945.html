Design. Against the training argument:

* It's been 2 years.

* I'm riding upright more than recumbent.

* I have SPDs on both, so I suspect my pedaling action is similar for both. (Notably I pull on both.)

For the design argument:

* I feel the wind much less on the recumbent.

* the upright is a very-upright hybrid. I might get better results with a "real" road bike.
