Not only was it most likely real, but the photo is not retouched, though it 
was "staged" most assuredly. Keith's was a chain of Vaudeville theaters, 
later to be movie houses. Trick riding was a staple of such shows at the time. 
Stopping motion photographically with flash powder was quite common to the 
era as well, though for this shot it probably took   a set of well placed 
flash pans   with reflectors to get the flat lighting.
