After I developed some heart problems that limited my heart rate and wattage output, I put a Bionx pedelec system on my faired Challenge Hurricane.  My 10 mile commute time is back down to 35 min so I am riding more often.  Great for fast starts and hills, but governed to 20 mph max assist.  Probably fast enough anyway...



As for uprights, I have a Brompton in my office for quick short trips around campus and a Gazelle omafiets for stylish trips to the market.  But I wouldn't consider a "head first" for my bike trail commute; the risks of a header among the pedestrians are too great.
