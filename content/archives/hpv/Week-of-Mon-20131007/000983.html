John,

I have been riding my e-assist LWB recumbent for two years now, and am
amazed at the reaction from most people.  All my adult life I have had
people angry at me for using "their" road.  Many would shout, "Get a motor
on that thing!"  When I finally did, most peoples attitudes changed 180
degrees.:-)

Most motorcyclists, and classic car and hot rod types love it.  Good ole
boys, in pickup trucks, give me the thumbs up, and little kids shout, "Cool
bike mister!"

But, except for the kids, they say they could/would never ride one.

Tom,

Yes.  Last year we went to the Lane Motor Museum, in Nashville.

http://www.lanemotormuseum.org/

Probably the biggest collection of small and aero cars in the US...Tatras,
Messerschmidts, etc.

I have spent a lot of time on electric car sites since 2009.  I am
constantly amazed that most of the people there have no interest in
aerodynamics, or small vehicles.  They say stuff like, "The VW XL1 is
retro-ugly."  The worst insult they can think of is "golf cart."  All they
talk about is bigger, more powerful batteries.  They consider any
concession to efficiency, like rear fender skirts, a defeat for EV's.
