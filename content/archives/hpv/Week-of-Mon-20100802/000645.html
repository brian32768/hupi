I'm using kitty-litter buckets with a resealable lid on my bike. The
40 pound size will swallow huge amounts of cargo (including gallon
jugs of milk) with ample room and easily support 40 pounds of contents
(duh). They are easy to get via Freecycle as people with cats usually
just keep the first two or three buckets and then toss the rest.
