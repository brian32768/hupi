You have a fairing on the front of your Challenge Hurricane? I'd like  
to see a photo of that. I've never seen a front fairing on a  
Hurricane. That might be part of your vision problem right there. The  
fairing may be getting in the way of leaning forward to scan around  
parked cars at intersections. When I'm riding a low, laid back bike  
in traffic I lean forward in these situations to look around. Hard to  
do with the tiller steering common on Hurricanes but easy with USS or  
U-shaped OSS sometimes referred to as "open cockpit", "tweaners" or  
"superman bars".
