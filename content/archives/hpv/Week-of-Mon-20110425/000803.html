Or you could put a fisheye lens like the fotojojo ones on a smartphone.  Not that I have any direct experience with that product (yet), I've only just heard about it on the NY Times TechTimes podcast.

Also a trip-recording app like Dailyroads Voyager might give you valuable evidence in case of an accident (you or someone else).  But I can't wholeheartedly endorse this particular one yet- I've only just installed it and don't plan to use it much until I have a good mount for my smartphone.

But Dailyroads Voyager does display at the same time it records, and it records either stills or video clips, and it retains them on demand or on detection of a certain amount of G-force, and I would think a mounting for a smartphone at the head of your vehicle might do what you need with less weight and mounting complexity.  If battery life is not an issue (I charge my phone at both ends of my trips so it's not for me)
