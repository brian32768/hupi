In Colorado we have Bike to Work Day/Week in June.  It is not that we are wimps, but we can get a big snowstorm in May.  Despite being booked up with soccer practices and other things that force me to drive my car, I have managed to ride about once per week.  We had dry weather in January and February and that helped out.  It was so nice that I used the V2 Formula for commuting this winter instead of the police auction mtn bike.

I do love to see the interest in bike commuting.
