On the cheap side, Origin-8, a house brand of a national bike 
wholesaler, has 145, 155, and 165mm 130/74mm BCD cranks that retail for 
about $55 (square taper), though these are probably not stiff enough to 
take an athletic pounding.

There are lots of short BMX cranks with 110mm BCD, and one can easily 
find long chainring bolts that will hold three rings, and you can get 
110mm rings from 34 to 60 teeth.
