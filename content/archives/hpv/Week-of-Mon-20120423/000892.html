We had a phenomenal wipe-out at the Fl HPV race a few years back. Somebody not involved with the usual HPV bunch had designed a streamliner with front wheel drive and two back steer wheels. While flying high on the turn at the velodrome the steering gave way and the thing spun round and almost over the top. The driver/rider was not hurt but his ride was out of commission.
