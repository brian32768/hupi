If you put the downtube above the chainrings it reduces drag enough to gain about 1-mph average speed faster on the same wattage, a reason it was outlawed when track bikes used it and proved it worked to reduce drag significantly. 

This is bike-polo style, a well known frame design, uses slightly less metal, nothing else needs changing just the position of the downtube. 

Doing this to commuter bikes can save a lot of food if done globally, by the megatons, just from moving the one tube up.
