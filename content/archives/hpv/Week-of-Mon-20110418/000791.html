> If you want to hot rod around a bike is much more fun than a trike. But for
> alternate transportation the trike layout shines.

I rarely use any of my trikes in town for a simple reason: with a bike you can 
*always* get through traffic in time, as you can (almost) always overtake 
stopped traffic on the outside, or quickly dismount and push the bike past an 
obstruction on the sidewalk. A trike has a fixed minimum width and often there 
is less space than this. Also pushing on the sidewalk is more awkward.

Therefore I can guarantee timely arrival to the minute on a bike, but for a 
trike (or bike with trailer) I must allow some extra time in case of stopped 
traffic.
