Thanks to Zach, and Theo for your views on using tikes vs bikes. Good information. 

I have wondered how trikes work out in urban areas where traffic is really dense. 

In the average suburban towns in my area we have enough room for a trike. 
So I seldom ride a bike anymore. The car drivers treat my VM as a vehicle and often give me room. I can take a lane for a short while. 

I think part of this change in publics behavior comes from them seeing me running errands over the last 6 years in the VM and realizing that this is good for the  environment. They tell me that when they talk to me. 
People are slowly learning. 

I have the feeling that eventually there could be a market for VMs, not the racy looking type we have now but lighter more practical types set up for average folks.
