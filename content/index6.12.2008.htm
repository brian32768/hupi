On 5th December Al Krause of the IHPVA (the Californian version) took control of the registration for the domain name "IHPVA.org" in a hostile takeover against the wishes of the IHPVA (international version), owner of the domain name. The registrar "godaddy.com" forked it over in less than an hour without any advance notice to the legal registrants Richard Ballantine or Brian Wilson. We recommend anyone who has a domain to protect that is using this company's service to move it.

There is at present a mirror on http://www.whpva.org but Al Krause has demanded control of this as well and anyway countless links of archival material may break in the future. You may be able to find pre-December 2008 content on http://web.archive.org/web/*/http://ihpva.org We find the tactics of the Californian IHPVA deplorable and will now concentrate on content here at HuPI in the future.
